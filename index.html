Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our courses and enrollment process

Do I need any prior coding experience?

No prior experience is required! Our course is designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to more advanced topics. Many of our successful students started with zero coding knowledge.

How long does it take to complete the course?

The course is self-paced, but most students complete it in 3-6 months by dedicating 10-15 hours per week. You'll have lifetime access, so you can learn at your own pace and revisit materials whenever you need.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide multiple support channels including Q&A with instructors, a dedicated student community, technical support, and career coaching. You'll also get personalized code reviews for your projects to ensure you're on the right track.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes! You'll receive a verified certificate of completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume to showcase your skills to employers. Our certificates are recognized by industry partners.

What technologies will I learn?

You'll master HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Git, and more. We focus on the MERN stack which is highly sought after by employers. You'll also learn modern tools like TypeScript, Next.js, and GraphQL.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive career support including resume reviews, portfolio building, interview preparation, and connections to our hiring partners. Many of our students land jobs before even completing the course.
